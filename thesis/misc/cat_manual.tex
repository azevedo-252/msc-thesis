SYNOPSIS
  cat_abc [OPTION]...    [FILE]...
  cat_abc -s [OPTION]... [STRING]...

OPTIONS
  -s, --strmode
    ABC tunes are text streams instead of file names

  -d, --delta=I
    Measure rests of length I will be inserted at the beggining of each voice

  -r, --rep=I
    The tune will be repeated I times
